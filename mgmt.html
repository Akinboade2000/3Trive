Our Management Team

Experienced leadership driving our success

Engr. Kamar k. Bada

Executive Chairman

Kamar K. Bada is a seasoned professional with over 20 years of post-graduate experience in the upstream Oil and Gas industry...

Kamar K. Bada is a seasoned professional with over 20 years of post-graduate experience in the upstream Oil and Gas industry, culminating in senior project management and execution roles at ExxonMobil. His foundation in engineering was laid at Obafemi Awolowo University, where he earned a degree in Civil Engineering, further enhanced by an MSc in Oil & Gas Engineering from Robert Gordon University, Aberdeen.

During his distinguished career at ExxonMobil, Kamar navigated a diverse portfolio of projects, demonstrating strong leadership and execution capabilities. Following his successful tenure, Kamar transitioned his expertise into entrepreneurship, establishing 3trive Castles Ltd.

Kamal Adeola Adeniran

Chief Executive Officer

Kamal A. Adeniran is a seasoned site construction practitioner bringing over 15 years of hands-on experience to the built environment...

Kamal A. Adeniran is a seasoned site construction practitioner bringing over 15 years of hands-on experience to the built environment. As a co-founder and the Chief Executive Officer of 3trive Castle Ltd., a leading dredging and haulage company in Lagos State, Kamal has demonstrated exceptional leadership and a deep understanding of the critical logistical underpinnings of successful construction projects.

His extensive experience spans a diverse portfolio of construction activities, ranging from numerous building projects to significant road construction initiatives.
