About 3Trive Castles

Your trusted partner in sand dredging and land reclamation

Your Trusted Sand Dredging Partner

3Trive Castles Ltd is the leading sand dredging company in Lagos State, Nigeria. Our dredging site is located at Lagos and we have a fleet of dumping trucks that allow us to deliver sharp sand to customers across the state quickly and efficiently.

3Trive Castles Ltd is the leading sand dredging company in Lagos State, Nigeria, with our dredging site located at Lagasa. We operate a modern fleet of dumping trucks that enables us to deliver high-quality sharp sand to customers across the state quickly and efficiently. Our company is built on a foundation of reliability, quality assurance, and customer satisfaction, offering competitive pricing while maintaining rigorous quality control processes.

At 3Trive Castles, we understand that time is money, which is why we prioritize prompt service delivery and accurate supply of sand requests. Our team of experienced professionals ensures every project meets exact specifications, making us the perfect choice for clients seeking trustworthy sand dredging services

Our Vision

Building Nigeria's Future

3Trive Castles Ltd envisions becoming Nigeria's premier sand dredging and land reclamation company, setting the industry standard for quality, reliability, and sustainable practices while contributing significantly to the nation's infrastructure development.

Our vision extends beyond simply supplying materials - we aim to be an integral partner in Nigeria's growth story, providing the foundational resources needed for construction projects that will shape the future of our communities and economy.

Through innovation, commitment to excellence, and sustainable practices, we strive to build not just structures, but lasting relationships and a better future for all Nigerians.
